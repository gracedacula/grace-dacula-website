Why We Give

Give Cheerfully

Your generosity makes a difference. Every gift helps us share the Gospel, serve our community, and support the mission of the church.

Each one must give as he has decided in his heart, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver.

— 2 Corinthians 9:7

Giving is secure and processed through Tithe.ly. You can give one-time or set up recurring giving below.

Other Ways to Give

More Options

By Check

Make checks payable to Grace Baptist Church of Dacula and bring them on Sunday or mail to our church address.

In Person

Offering is received during Sunday worship. Envelopes are available at the welcome table if you prefer privacy.

Questions?
