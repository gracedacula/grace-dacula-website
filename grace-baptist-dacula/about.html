Our Story

A Church for
Every Person

Clear, expositional preaching of the Word of God is the heartbeat of Grace Baptist Church — and so is the community that gathers around it.

Grace Dacula is a story of God's varied grace. Since our beginning in the year 2000, our story at Grace Baptist Church of Dacula is a reminder that God's grace is sufficient for all.

We are all different — Southerners, Northerners, immigrants, Native Georgians. We speak many languages and come from many walks of life. But we have one thing in common: a desire to know and love God, and to serve Him with all our heart.

We enjoy being together. Our fellowship is sweet. The story of Grace Baptist is a story of a wonderful family fellowship — people loving other imperfect people the way Christ Jesus loves us. We would love for you to visit and we hope that our church family might become yours!

What We Believe

Rooted in Scripture

We hold to the historic Baptist faith and affirm the authority of Scripture in all matters of faith and practice. These are the doctrines that anchor our worship, our community, and our mission.

Full Statement of Faith →
01
The Trinity

We believe in one God in three persons: God the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

02
Scripture

We believe the Bible to be inspired by God in the original manuscripts — the inerrant, infallible Word of God and the final authority for faith and practice.

03
Creation & Sin

We believe God created the earth as revealed in Genesis, that Adam and Eve were made in His image, and that their willful sin left mankind totally unable to remedy his own lost condition.

04
Redemption

We believe the Lord Jesus Christ accomplished the Father's eternal purpose through His substitutionary death on the cross, verified by His literal, physical resurrection from the grave.

05
Salvation

We believe salvation is the gift of God's righteousness, received by personal faith in Jesus Christ expressed in repentant confession of Him as Lord and Savior.

06
The Holy Spirit

We believe the Holy Spirit convicts, guides believers into truth, and is the supernatural agent in regeneration — baptizing, indwelling, and sealing believers until the day of redemption.

07
The Church

We believe the local church is comprised of born-again believers commissioned to make disciples of all nations, baptizing them and teaching them to observe all that Christ commanded.

08
The Ordinances

We believe in two ordinances for the church that testify of the completed work of Christ: baptism and communion.

09
Christ's Return

We believe in the premillennial, imminent return of the Lord Jesus Christ for the redeemed.

10
Resurrection & Eternity

We believe in the resurrection of all men — the saved to eternal life in a literal heaven, and the unsaved to eternal judgment for rejecting Jesus Christ.
