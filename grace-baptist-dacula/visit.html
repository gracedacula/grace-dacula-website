Plan a Visit

We know showing up somewhere new can feel awkward — here's everything you need to know before Sunday.

01
Arrival & Parking

We're located at 2980 Old Peachtree Rd NE, Dacula, GA 30019. Greeters will welcome you at the door and help you find your way.

02
The Worship Service

Clear, expositional preaching of God's Word is the heartbeat of Grace Baptist. Pastor Kimbrough leads our Sunday service with vibrant singing and Bible-centered teaching. Children's Worship runs at the same time for younger ones.
